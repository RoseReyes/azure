About Me

I love to partake in challenging journey because the aftermath is rewarding. I have always wanted to build technological applications ever since I graduated from a CS major 10 years ago.

I worked as a Business and System Analysis for 5 years, realized the job is not challenging enough, so I enrolled in a bootcamp and gain a fresh take on new methods, concepts of application development using 3 Stacks in Python, C# and MEAN.

Out of all the 3, C# and MEAN Full stack development really caught my interest. During the bootcamp, I was able to create simple yet robust applications with full front and backend functionalities with these technologies. Being a woman in this Industry is a struggle but I was able to finish as a strong, self-sufficient developer and made a life-long mission to excel in this craft.

I'm not a coding rockstar but I always get my stuff done. I would love to join a team/organization/company having the same goals that I can grow and share my passion with.

When I'm not coding I love to explore the outdoors, be a foodie and have fun.
